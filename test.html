About Factional Warfare

Factional Warfare is a conflict within warzones fought by corporations aligned with one of the four empires of New Eden.

For members of a Factional Warfare corporation, all regions of empire space (hisec & lowsec) become areas where you and your faction allies may freely attack pilots from enemy militias without CONCORD interference.

Participants fight to capture warzone systems for their faction.
